WAN port doesn't work?

Instead of replacing your router, You can try to convert one of the LAN ports into a WAN Port. This may or may not work for you depending on the model of your router, and availability of a firmware that you can install on it. But, it is something to consider if you don't feel like throwing out a functioning router just because of a faulty WAN port.

Besides, if converting a LAN port into a WAN port doesn't work for you, you can always use your Router as a Wireless Access Point (AP) to improve Wifi coverage along with your new router or as a Switch to connect your devices via a LAN cable for a fast reliable physical link.
